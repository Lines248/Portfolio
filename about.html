About

I'm Lines, a front-end developer and creative technologist focused on accessible, clear, and thoughtful interaction. My background is in design, installation work, and creative tech, where I built physical systems, objects, and environments. That experience shaped how I think about structure, material, and the way people move through an experience.

After a studio accident and the onset of CRPS, I shifted fully into software. Coding became another material to work with, and accessibility became the foundation of my approach. Today I build modern interfaces with semantic HTML, clean architecture, and inclusive interaction patterns.

My work centers on clarity and usability. I focus on accessible front-end development, modern JavaScript, and design-led engineering, bringing precision, care, and a sense of craft into the things I build.
